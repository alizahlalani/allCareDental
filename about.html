Dr. Yoon Kim, DMD

Dentist

Dr. Kim grew up in Anchorage, Alaska, and moved to Texas to pursue her undergraduate degree at Baylor University in Waco, TX. She attended Midwestern University College of Dental Medicine in Glendale, AZ, and then moved east for an advanced general dentistry residency in Buffalo, NY at University at Buffalo. She has practiced in New York, California and now Texas. Dr. Kim enjoys creating beautiful healthy smiles through comfortable, convenient care for the whole family.

Dr. Kim focuses on restorative and cosmetic dentistry, and enjoys working with children and adults. She is a member of the Texas Dental Association as well as American Dental Association.
